San Diego Chapter Continuing Education Program

A Continuing Education Fund has been established for the sole use of its Members and their Employee Members. The purpose of the fund is to financially assist those Members who are committed to improving their technical expertise through the attendance of technical training in the pool service industry.

​

The Education Fund will be funded from the Manufacturer's Rebate Program and selling Products received.(Note: Income from Chapter Supporters and Fines will be added to the General Fund.)

​

Chapter Members may request the fund for any type of seminar, course, license program or training which is directly related to the technical or business aspect of pool and spa service.

​

Applications

Continuing Education Reimbursement Application

Continuing Education Points Program and Form

  • Applications must be received by the Continuing Education chair within 30 days of attending and completing seminars and courses.
  • Receipt or proof of attendance and course payment must be submitted to Education Committee Chairman before reimbursement will be made.

Applications for the Education Fund will be evaluated on the following basis:

  • Benefit to the pool/spa industry
  • Benefit to the Chapter

Based upon the availability of funds and the evaluation criteria above, below is the schedule of reimbursement:

  • Local classes (San Diego County): 100% of class cost
  • SoCal area: 100% of class/show cost + $250 for expenses
  • Outside SoCal: 100% of class/show cost + $400 for expenses
  • NOTE: $1,200 maximum per year for each Regular member. $600 maximum per year total all Employee Members of each Regular Member Must provide proof of attendance in addition to proof of course payment. Submit application for reimbursement within 30 days of attendance or completion.
  • Contractors License: 100% of non-recurring expenses (classes, application/test fees, fingerprint charge) up to $1,000 max
